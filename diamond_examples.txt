 1. Basic Print & Variables:

mera dost x = 10
mera dost y = 20

sun dost x
sun dost y
sun dost x + y
  
 Output:
  10
  20
  30


 2. Simple Function Definition & Call:

kaam dost add(a, b)
    wapas dost a + b
bas dost

sun dost bula dost add(3, 5)

 Output:
  8

 3. If-Else Control Flow:

mera dost age = 18

agar dost age >= 18
    sun dost "You are an adult"
warna dost
    sun dost "You are a minor"
bas dost

 Output:
  You are an adult

 4. While Loop:

mera dost i = 1

jab tak dost i <= 3
    sun dost i
    mera dost i = i + 1
bas dost
 
 Output:
  1
  2
  3

5. For Loop with Steps:

ghum dost i se 1 tak 5 step 2
    sun dost i
bas dost

 Output:
  1
  3
  5

6. Function with Inner Logic:

kaam dost multiply(a, b)
    mera dost result = a * b
    wapas dost result
bas dost

sun dost bula dost multiply(4, 6)

  Output:
   24

7. Nested Function Calls:

kaam dost square(x)
    wapas dost x * x
bas dost

kaam dost cube(x)
    wapas dost x * bula dost square(x)
bas dost

sun dost bula dost cube(2)

  Output:
   8

8. Function Returning Function Output:

kaam dost greet(name)
    wapas dost "Hello " + name
bas dost

sun dost bula dost greet("Priyanshu")

 Output:
  Hello Priyanshu

9. Conditional Inside Loop:

ghum dost i se 1 tak 5 step 1
    agar dost i % 2 == 0
        sun dost i + " is even"
    warna dost
        sun dost i + " is odd"
    bas dost
bas dost

  Output:
   1 is odd
   2 is even
   3 is odd
   4 is even
   5 is odd
